Frequently Asked Questions:

How long do you have to wait for orders to be delivered?
Customers can find there delivery date in the order confirmation email sent to customers upon purchases.

Can we request refunds?
Customers can only request refunds until 48 hours after the order confirmation email is sent. After this duration, customers are unable to request refunds.

What happens if my order arrives damaged?
If orders are arrived damaged, customers have to provide proof that the damage was not caused by them. If the customer successfully proves that they did not cause the damage, they can either request a full refund or a new product.
