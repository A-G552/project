Getting to Know Us

What is Sustainable Store about? What are our goals? Why does sustainable buying matter?

About Us

Sustainable Store is guided by the 3 R's, Reduce, Reuse and Recycle. We are disciplined in following what we think is right for the people and for the environment. We are able to change the world but only with your help. Be Eco-Friendly and live sustainable lives to be part of our journey into making the world a better place.

The Impact of Pollution

Pollution is a significant problem within South Africa and around the globe. Pollution such as material waste, plastic waste and air pollution all contribute to significant, negative environmental problems. These problems include the negative impact of ocean and land wildlife as well as negatively impacting the economy.

Our Goal

Our goal as Sustainable Store is to create an online platform to promote and encourage to change the world's culture to be more sustainable. We are here to provide a space for the buying of upcycled and eco-friendly products as well as providing the opportunity for our customers to donate any recyclable material that they have lying around in their homes. By using our plastic donation services and purchasing products from our store, you are not only helping yourselves but also the planet. Together we can make a change.
