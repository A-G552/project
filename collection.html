Donation and Collection Details

Customers who opt to donate plastic after purchasing one of our listed products, they will be able to donate their plastic when the product that they have purchased is delivered. This will be free of charge and all the customer needs to do is have their plastic stored in a bag so that it is ready for the delivery service to collect.

Customers who opt to donate plastic but have not made a purchase will be required to pay a small collection fee of R15. The reason why it is not free is because part of the money received from product purchases goes towards paying the delivery drivers.
